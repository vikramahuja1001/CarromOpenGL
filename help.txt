The game consists of 4 white , 4 black and 1 red coin. The striker color is randomly determined to be either black or white. If it's color is white then it is only suppose to play the coins and vice-versa.
A speedometer has been implemented on the right side of the carrom board.
Tha carrom will move on the line with the help of 'Left'/ 'Right' key, speed will increase/ decrease by 'up'/'down' key and the angle will be set by 'a'/'c' keys
